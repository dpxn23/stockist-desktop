The Ingenue

The Ingenue

The Ingenue is a boutique shop selling handpoured soy candles. Located at 999 Bathurst Street, owners and life-partners Mike and Becca have been running the business for 5 years. Their bestselling items include Jasmine, Gardenia and Spearmint candles.


Available for delivery & curbside pick-up.


NBHD Chocolate

NBHD Chocolate

Mathieu moved to Toronto 10 years ago from Paris where he studied under the best pastry chefs on the planet. Now, he makes exquisite chocolates in Little Italy with flavours ranging from spicy cherry to mango-mangosteen. His storefront is located at 123 College Street.


Available for curbside pick-up only.


Milky's

Milky's

Milky's is a Toronto café that serves thoughtfully crafted coffee and seasonal specialty drinks. Our flavours are unconventional, their presentation is considered and their interior is cozy. You can find them at 555 Dundas St. W.


Available for curbside pick-up and delivery.
